About Us

SheFAB is a Joint Enterprise of the Fintech and Block Chain Association (FAB) and SheFanPro. It’s a fully evolved Blockchain ecosystem with more than 40,000 developers coming together to bridge the gender gap, alleviate poverty, and ultimately progress toward building a more inclusive world.

Opportunities

Complimentary Subscription to SheFAB exclusive resources

Chapter Members are eligible for a complimentary electronic resource of Fintech and Blockchain technology in PDF’s, files and E-books and will be able to learn from worldwide renowned Masters.

Conferences

Chapter members are eligible to attend all seminars and conferences without any payment, and are also eligible to receive certificates which will enhance their CV.

Knowledge sharing and Networking

Chapter members will get the expose of and connect with professional networks which may give them opportunity of internships or may get the opportunity of getting recruited by reputed organizations.

Explore career opportunities

Opportunity to link students with Corporate world, with the aim of providing them with professional environment wherein they may get opportunity to work on various live projects.

Make contacts that will last a lifetime

Corporate Networking and Academic Collaborations which SheFAB platform provides to it's members gives them an opportunity to associate and interact with esteemed organizations and business leaders. These counselling may come from global leaders even.

Develop leadership skills

Share ideas and enthusiasm with fellow students and faculties from all relevant departments. Provide them technical and social projects according to their skills and ability in order to prepare them for future opportunities.
